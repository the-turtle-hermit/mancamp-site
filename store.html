Storefront

Easy to configure. Easy to grow.

This page is built for clean product cards now and simple checkout links later, without forcing you into a heavy store platform on day one.

How this works

Good fit for a budget-friendly launch

Each product card uses an image, name, price, short description, and buy button. When you are ready, each button can point to a Stripe Payment Link or another checkout path.

That gives you a legitimate e-commerce setup without overcomplicating the project or raising the ongoing cost too early.
