What is Man Camp South?

Mission

A place for men to grow deeper in Christ alongside other men.

Man Camp South is being built around fellowship, biblical teaching, and intentional community. The goal is not a crowded or flashy experience. It is a direct and meaningful environment where men can step away from routine, listen well, connect deeply, and return home more grounded.

The site stays simple on purpose: Home for the overview, About for the mission, and Details for the event logistics and registration path.

A

Fellowship

Meaningful connection with other men in a setting that encourages real conversation and intentional community.

B

Biblical teaching

Teaching and service moments designed to keep the camp centered on Jesus Christ and spiritual growth.

C

Purposeful environment

An outdoors-forward weekend with room for reflection, structure, brotherhood, and a practical next step toward deeper faith.

Guest speaker

Jonnie W.

Jonnie W. blends music, off-beat standup, and a clean, family-friendly live comedy style. His bio notes performances in 48 states, regular SiriusXM play, and a Dry Bar Comedy special with more than 5 million views.

He is known for mixing stand-up, storytelling, and music into a set that is memorable, approachable, and a strong fit for a weekend built around encouragement, fellowship, and faith.
