Mission

Purpose, brotherhood, and hope.

Man Camp South is a weekend experience designed to bring men together through the outdoors, real conversations, and strong community. Our mission is to create a place where men can step away from the stress and noise of everyday life, build meaningful connections, grow personally and spiritually, and encourage one another to become stronger leaders, better men, and true disciples in their everyday lives. We especially want to reach the men who feel forgotten, disconnected, or far from having a solid relationship with Jesus — reminding them that they are not alone and that there is still purpose, brotherhood, and hope waiting for them.
